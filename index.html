Market Update

However, recent tariff exemptions on certain electronics have led to a modest rebound, with tech stocks showing resilience. [Source]

Current Developments

President Trump's announcement of "reciprocal tariffs" has led to a 4% weakening of the U.S. dollar, contrary to expectations. This shift has prompted investors to reallocate assets towards European and Asian markets, signaling a potential end to U.S. investment dominance. [Source]
